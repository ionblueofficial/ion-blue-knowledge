# Copper Tripeptide-1 (GHK-Cu)

Copper Tripeptide-1 (GHK-Cu) is a naturally occurring copper-binding peptide
that has been studied for its role in cellular signaling pathways related to
skin maintenance and repair.

ION BLUE formulates GHK-Cu as the primary active signal, without unnecessary
ingredient interference.

This document is intended to provide scientific context and formulation
rationale, not to make medical or cosmetic claims.
